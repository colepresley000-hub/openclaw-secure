Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between ShieldClaw (free) and Monitor?
ShieldClaw (free) provides deployment-time security with configuration templates, setup scripts, and manual tools. ShieldClaw Monitor adds 24/7 runtime monitoring, automated drift detection, real-time alerts, and a web dashboard to continuously protect your production deployments.
Do I need to use the free version before Monitor?
Yes! ShieldClaw Monitor builds on top of the free ShieldClaw toolkit. You'll first deploy using the free setup wizard and configuration templates, then add Monitor for continuous protection.
Can I try Monitor before paying?
Absolutely! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You'll get full access to all Monitor features during the trial.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Monitor runs on your infrastructure as a daemon and only sends anonymized metrics for dashboard display. We never see your configuration files, API keys, or sensitive data. Enterprise customers can opt for completely on-premise deployment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice/bank transfer with annual contracts.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your Monitor subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
Do you offer discounts for startups or nonprofits?
Yes! YC-backed startups get 50% off Monitor for 12 months. Nonprofits get 30% off. Contact us with proof of status to claim your discount.
